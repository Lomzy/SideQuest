Step 1: Meta Website - Create Organisation

To allow installing apps & games to your headset we need to enable developer mode. To do this, you have to be a registered "developer" on the Meta website, it's free, quick and easy. Open dashboard.oculus.com and create an 'organization'. You'll need to accept the developer agreement and to verify your account using either a credit card or a phone number. Most accounts will already be verified. Next you'll need to create an organisation like above.

Once you have enabled developer mode it is a good idea to reboot your headset to be able to see it on the device.

Step 3: Connect USB Cable and Allow USB Debugging

Now its time to connect the USB cable. Once you are connected you will have to allow USB debugging access on your headset.
